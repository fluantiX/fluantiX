How To Use WeatherXTR

Step 1: On the homepage of this website, click on the expo image. This will redirect you to Expo store.
Step 2: Click on the blue button from expo store texted Install.
Step 3: You will get a warning that looks like that of the image below, The warning is shown because the app is not from play store. Click on ok to continue.
Step 4: Once the app is downloaded, tap on the "Open" button to launch it.
Step 5: You you get a screen pop up that will look of that below, Click install.
Step 6: After clicking on Install, you will have a new error which points that the app and the developer of the app is not available on Google play store. Click on more details.
Step 7: Once the more details is clicked, you will have a button texted Install anyways, Click on it and the app will be Installed Successfully.

Note: Our app will never ask for permissions other than your location, which is necessary to retrieve data for results.
